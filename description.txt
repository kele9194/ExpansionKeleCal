Requires both Kele Expansion and Calamity Mod to use this mod.
This mod is developed for entertainment purposes only, and the developer is just me, still in the process of improvement.
This mod is a Calamity expansion version of Kele Expansion, designed for playing with Kele Expansion and Calamity mainly as post-Moon Lord item expansion.
Currently, this mod contains the following series:
Phantom Stary Sword series and Phantom Stary Sniper Rifle series, totaling 20 Phantom Stary series weapons;
Phantom Stary Armor series with 10 armor sets, plus one accessory (that's right, there's only this many for now, more Calamity-related items will be added in future updates).
(Art assets are reused due to lack of artistic skills)
In addition, this mod does not rewrite any existing boss AI, so it can be added after world creation.
If you have any other ideas or suggestions, feel free to leave a comment.
